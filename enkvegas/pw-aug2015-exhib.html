Booth Graphics & Furnishings

All graphics must be approved by the PROJECT WOMENS show team, please complete the Furniture and Graphic Approval Request to submit your custom graphics and furnishings to the show team. Once approved, graphics must be printed by GES or through one of our ENK approved vendors (if you choose to use a vendor other than GES, you will still be charged for the labor to install and dismantle your graphic. Please see the guidebook for specific rules and regulations).

Graphics are only permitted within the booth space; no graphics are allowed to be hung on the outside walls of the booth. The below diagram indicates the areas in your booth that graphics are permitted.

All custom Graphics and Furniture must be approved by ENK prior to production or installation. The graphic approval deadline is July 24.

For more information, please contact GES at Kjefferson@ges.com or operations@enkshows.com with any further questions.

*Please keep in mind turnaround time to produce fabric graphics is approximately 48 hours, orders received onsite may not be produced or install prior to show. All fabric graphics orders through GES can be reused for future shows. For storage prices through GES, please contact them directly at 800.801.0554.
